Marinsa

Marinsa provides specialized vessels for the offshore industry. We have type boats: DP1 and DP2, AHTS, Tugboats, Suppliers, Utility and FSV, as well as vessels for seismic, geophysical, geotechnical, mud, inspection, among others.

We provide rig movements: Jack Up's, modular and semi-submersible. We transport personnel, materials, equipment, water and diesel offshore. Our base is located in Ciudad del Carmen and we operate throughout the Gulf of Mexico. We have offices in Houston, Bogota and Singapore.

  • Geophysics and Geotechnics
  • Subsea Projects
  • Pipelines and Lines Layout
  • Movement of platforms
